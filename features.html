Features

Make Every Design Different

Fed up with frameworks that make every website look and feel like a framework? You want a custom design, so you override defaults. Every time again. You have grown out of that. You are ready to leave Bootstrap or Foundation behind. Time to get proper support for your slick design skills.

A Grid for Every Site

Want to build a complex responsive news website, or just want to make a one-page design? Enter the grid. Chopstick gives you the possibility to use either a fixed or fluid lay-out. You can easily configure the number of colums and gutter widths, and make your page lay-out perfect on every device. 

.blog-detail {
@include box-(12, 1, 1);
// Span 12 columns
}

@media (min-width: $bp1) {
.blog-detail {
	@include box-(6, 1, 1);
	// Span 6 columns
}
}

Semantics Like a Boss

You know the right class names and HTML hierarchy are essential when making future friendly sites and complex applications. Good semantics help your fellow designers and developers understand your code. With Chopstick, you use the naming conventions you love. We get out of the way.

Typography with Meaning

The era of digital typography has finally arrived. Almost every typeface is at our disposal for use on screen. But with great power comes great responsibility.

Text contains hierarchy. And you want to create hierarchy in a hassle-free and meaningful way. Including the latest font is not enough. You want to find a way to incorperate the golden ratio in your headers and paragraph text. Chopstick gets you going with a little help from our friend modular scale. 

.title {
	font-size: ms(3);
	// Up 3 steps in modular scale
}

Navigation Patterns

You recognise a well designed website by its navigation. Chopstick contains the basics to create horizontal or list-style navigation. For your responsive design we have provided a clean mobile navigation example.

More navigational inspiration? Check out some of the work of Brad Frost at his Pattern Lab. Don’t stick to our examples. Go create!

Alzheimer-proof Boilerplate

Some things are required in every site you make. It can be a pain to remember what meta-tags, conditional comments or favicon files you need. Chopstick is delivered with some nice boilerplate files to kick-start your project. Like Alzeheimer’s medicine.
